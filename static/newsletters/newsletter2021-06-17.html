Don't let slowdowns get you down

This year many of my customers who work from home experienced productivity concerns with their computers or laptops. Many performance and stability issues are the result of out-of-date hardware. These problems usually pop up over a five to seven-year period of time and get worse over time. If you are noticing a major slow down in the operation of your device, you might need an upgrade, or, more importantly, it could be a storage device failure that can result in data loss.
