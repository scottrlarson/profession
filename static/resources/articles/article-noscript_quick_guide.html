Date:

Published:
No
Category:

For:
PC computer users that want to protect their operating system from arbitrary running of scripts.

NOSCRIPT is a plugin for the Firefox Web browser. It protects your computer from potentially dangerous scripts executing without your permission. This guide will show you how to interact with the options when you visit a web page that NOSCRIPT is preventing you from using.
